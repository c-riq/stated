Automatic Publishing

When you click "Submit", the editor will automatically:

  1. Fetch and update /.well-known/statements.txt
  2. Create /.well-known/statements/<hash>.txt
  3. Update /.well-known/statements/index.txt
  4. Upload any attachments to /.well-known/statements/attachments/
  5. Invalidate cache for immediate updates
